## 16. Before You Publish: Final Review

1. **Proofread everything**
   - Check for typos and grammatical errors
   - Ensure dates match your resume exactly
   - Verify all links work

2. **Consistency check**
   - Compare to your resume - should match exactly
   - Check that metrics are consistent (60% YoY, 200K+ users, etc.)
   - Ensure company names and titles match

3. **Test on mobile**
   - View profile on LinkedIn mobile app
   - Ensure formatting looks good
   - Check that banner and photo display well

4. **Privacy settings**
   - Decide if you want profile updates to notify your network
   - Consider turning off activity broadcasts during big updates
