## Overview

This guide provides specific recommendations for optimizing your LinkedIn profile for job search visibility. Each section includes current best practices, recommended content, and implementation steps.

## 9. Recommendations

**Best Practice**: Have 3-5 recommendations from different types of colleagues (managers, peers, cross-functional partners)

**Ideal Recommendation Mix:**
- 1-2 from former managers (speaks to your performance and growth)
- 1-2 from product managers or engineers (shows cross-functional collaboration)
- 1 from a peer designer (demonstrates your craft and teamwork)

**Recommendation Request Template:**

```
Subject: LinkedIn Recommendation Request

Hi [Name],

I hope you're doing well! I'm currently updating my LinkedIn profile as I explore new opportunities, and I'd be honored if you'd be willing to write a brief recommendation.

We worked together on [specific project or time period], and I really valued [specific thing you appreciated about working together - their mentorship, our collaboration, the project outcome, etc.].

If you're comfortable writing a recommendation, it would be great if you could touch on:
‚Ä¢ [Specific skill or strength - e.g., "my ability to collaborate with engineering"]
‚Ä¢ [Specific project or outcome - e.g., "the design system work we did together"]
‚Ä¢ [Any specific impact - e.g., "how the work improved team velocity"]

I know you're busy, so no pressure at all - and I'd be happy to return the favor with a recommendation for you as well.

Thanks for considering!

Best,
Gabe
```

**Who to Ask:**
- Former managers from Integrate, AfterPromCentral, NYC New Years projects
- Product managers you've collaborated with closely
- Engineers who worked on implementing your designs
- Fellow designers who can speak to your craft and process

**Implementation Steps:**
1. Make a list of 5-7 people to request recommendations from
2. Personalize the template above for each person
3. Send requests via LinkedIn or email (email is often more personal)
4. Follow up after 1 week if no response
5. Thank them and offer to reciprocate

## 16. Before You Publish: Final Review

Before making your profile public with all updates:

1. **Proofread everything**
   - Check for typos and grammatical errors
   - Ensure dates are consistent and accurate
   - Verify all links work

2. **Get feedback**
   - Ask a trusted colleague to review
   - Check that your story is clear and compelling
   - Ensure metrics are accurate and verifiable

3. **Test on mobile**
   - View your profile on LinkedIn mobile app
   - Ensure formatting looks good
   - Check that banner and photo display well

4. **Privacy check**
   - Review privacy settings
   - Decide if you want profile updates to notify your network
   - Consider turning off activity broadcasts during big updates

5. **Align with other materials**
   - Ensure LinkedIn aligns with your resume
   - Match portfolio website messaging
   - Consistent personal branding across platforms
