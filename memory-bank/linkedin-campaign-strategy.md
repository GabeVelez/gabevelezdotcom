# LinkedIn Job Search Campaign Strategy

## Campaign Overview

**Goal**: Position Gabe Velez as a senior product designer with 10+ years of experience who transforms complex problems into elegant, user-centered solutions.

**Target Audience**:
- Hiring managers at startups and mid-size companies
- Recruiters in tech/SaaS space
- Design leaders and VPs of Product
- Product managers and engineering leaders

**Timeline**: 4-6 weeks of active posting (3-4 posts per week)

**Key Differentiators**:
- Deep cross-functional collaboration (design, product, engineering)
- Data-driven design decisions
- Design systems expertise
- Rapid prototyping and implementation
- Strong technical understanding

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. Case Study Deep Dives (40%)
Share specific projects with measurable impact. Each case study gets 2-3 posts.

### 2. Process & Methodology (30%)
Behind-the-scenes look at how you work, your design process, tools, and frameworks.

### 3. Lessons Learned (20%)
Insights from 10+ years in the field - what works, what doesn't, mistakes made and lessons learned.

### 4. Industry Insights (10%)
Commentary on design trends, tools, and the evolving role of product design.

---

## Posting Strategy

### Frequency
**3-4 posts per week** (Monday, Wednesday, Friday, +optional Sunday)

### Best Times to Post
- Monday: 8-9 AM ET (week kickoff energy)
- Wednesday: 12-1 PM ET (lunch scroll)
- Friday: 9-10 AM ET (before weekend wind-down)
- Sunday: 6-7 PM ET (Sunday scaries job search time)

### Post Format Guidelines
- **Hook**: First 1-2 lines must grab attention (shows in feed preview)
- **Length**: 150-200 words ideal (long enough for depth, short enough to read)
- **Visuals**: Always include an image, video, or carousel
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant hashtags max
- **CTA**: End with a question or call-to-action to drive engagement

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Actions (15-20 min/day)
1. **Respond to all comments** on your posts within 24 hours
2. **Comment meaningfully** on 3-5 posts from:
   - Design leaders you admire
   - Companies you're interested in
   - Recruiters in your network
3. **Share relevant articles** with your take (2-3 per week)

### Weekly Actions (30-60 min/week)
1. **Connect with new people** (15-20 connection requests)
   - Designers at target companies
   - Recruiters who post design roles
   - People who engage with your content
2. **DM conversations** with warm leads
   - Thank people who share your posts
   - Reach out to recruiters who comment
3. **Engage with company pages** you're interested in
   - Like and comment on their posts
   - Share relevant content from them

### Connection Request Template
```
Hi [Name], I came across your profile and was impressed by [specific thing about their work/company]. I'm a product designer with 10+ years of experience in [relevant area], and I'd love to connect and learn more about [their work/company/field]. Looking forward to connecting!
```

## Notes & Considerations

### Authenticity
- Be genuine and conversational, not salesy
- Share real challenges and lessons, not just wins
- Let your personality show through

### Consistency
- Post regularly - consistency matters more than perfection
- If you miss a day, don't stress - just pick up where you left off
- Quality > quantity, but regular presence is important

### Patience
- LinkedIn algorithms favor consistent posters
- It may take 2-3 weeks to build momentum
- Engagement begets more engagement

### Iteration
- Pay attention to what resonates
- Double down on content types that work
- Adjust strategy based on real feedback
