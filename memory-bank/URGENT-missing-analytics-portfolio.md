## Case Study Content Outline

**Title:** ListenLoop Analytics Dashboard - Account Intelligence for B2B Sales

**Subtitle:** Transforming complex visitor tracking data into actionable insights for ABM platform

**The Challenge:**
B2B sales teams needed to identify high-value accounts visiting their website, but existing analytics tools (Google Analytics) didn't provide account-level intelligence or visitor identification.

**My Role:**
Principal Product Designer (sole designer) - UX research, wireframing, prototyping, visual design, frontend collaboration

**The Solution:**

1. **Account Intelligence Dashboard**
   - Visitor-to-account matching algorithm visualization
   - Engagement scoring system (hot/warm/cold accounts)
   - Visitor behavior timeline and page view tracking
   - Account-level conversion funnel analytics

2. **Multi-Touch Attribution Reporting**
   - Campaign attribution across multiple touchpoints
   - Channel performance comparison
   - Conversion path visualization

3. **Agency Mode: Multi-Account Management**
   - Unified dashboard for 10-50+ client accounts
   - Comparative analytics across accounts
   - Client-level permissions and white-labeling

4. **Web Personalization Tool**
   - Rule builder for dynamic content
   - A/B testing for personalized experiences
   - Real-time preview of personalization logic

**The Outcome:**
- Enabled sales teams to prioritize outreach based on account engagement data
- Reduced time-to-insight from days (manual analysis) to seconds (dashboard view)
- Agency customers could manage 50+ clients efficiently from single interface
- Product became core differentiator in competitive ABM market

**What I Learned:**
- Designing for "data scientists who aren't data scientists" (sales teams)
- Balancing data density with usability (how much to show vs. hide)
- Multi-tenancy UX patterns for agency/enterprise products
- Collaborating with data engineers on visualization performance
