## üö® Red Flags to Watch For

### If you're NOT getting phone screens after fixing positioning:

**Possible issues:**
1. **Still being filtered by ATS**
   - Solution: Add more keywords from job descriptions to resume
   - Solution: Apply directly on company sites (not LinkedIn Easy Apply)

2. **Resume format issues**
   - Solution: Use plain text resume, no fancy formatting
   - Solution: Ensure resume is ATS-parseable (test with Jobscan)

3. **LinkedIn/Resume mismatch**
   - Solution: Ensure LinkedIn and resume tell same story (recruiters check both)

4. **Overqualification still showing through**
   - Solution: Remove more management/leadership language
   - Solution: Emphasize "IC" more explicitly in headline

5. **Wrong roles targeted**
   - Solution: Only apply to "Senior Product Designer" or "Staff Product Designer" (not Principal/Director)
   - Solution: Avoid "Lead" titles (implies management)

---

### If you're getting phone screens but not progressing:

**Possible issues:**
1. **Interview skills rusty** (you haven't interviewed in years)
   - Solution: Practice with Exponent, Pramp, or friend mock interviews
   - Solution: Record yourself answering common questions

2. **Portfolio case studies need work**
   - Solution: Expand TokTix case study (most impressive work)
   - Solution: Add more business metrics to existing case studies

3. **Coming across as overqualified in conversations**
   - Solution: Emphasize "I want to stay hands-on with code"
   - Solution: Downplay leadership experience

4. **Age discrimination** (interviewers see you're 49)
   - Solution: Can't fully control this, but emphasize modern tools (AI, React Native)
   - Solution: Show energy and enthusiasm (not "I'm winding down my career")
